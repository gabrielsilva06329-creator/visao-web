A Arte · The Craft

Threads, not prints.

Every VISÃO piece is embroidered, not printed. Stitched line by line in heavyweight thread on heavyweight cotton — the kind of work that takes hours per garment and lasts decades.

We work with one factory. We choose every thread color. We sign off on every sample. Each drop is limited because real embroidery can't be rushed.

Herança · The Story

It started with Lourdes.

VISÃO started with my grandmother, Lourdes. She came to the U.S. from Brazil, and she's the one who taught me what working with your hands really means.

The brand is named in her language. Every piece is embroidered, never printed — because that's how she'd want it made.

— Gabriel, founder
